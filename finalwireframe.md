# Wireframes / storyboards

From intense development in the ex-urbs, to new toll roads and train stations, and huge lifestyle centers being built, NOVA continues to change - not always for the better. Ultimately, I want to use the following metrics to argue for a more sustainable, compact development structure.

# User research 

## Target audience
My main audience is policymakers and the public in Northern Virginia. I ultimately would want this project to shine a light on poor patterns of development in the area, and promote more infill development in areas like Arlington and the inner-ring suburbs of Fairfax. To best capture my target audience, I asked people I know that have grown up in NOVA or have spent a significant amount of time there.
